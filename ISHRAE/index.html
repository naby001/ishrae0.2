Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness among our members about the vital field of HVAC&R. We are committed to enhancing the technical skills of students through hands-on training and expert-led workshops. We facilitate meaningful interactions among members, fostering a supportive community that encourages innovation. Furthermore, we actively promote collaborations with industry leaders, ensuring our members are well-prepared to tackle real-world challenges and make impactful contributions to the HVAC&R industry.

Our Activities

Our activities include organizing workshops, seminars, and webinars to educate students about HVAC&R, improve interaction, and foster a sense of community. We also conduct practical training sessions, industry visits, and participate in ISHRAE national competitions to enhance technical skills and promote healthy competition.
